About Us !

Welcome ! We are a team of programmers and fitness lovers based in Los Angeles, California. Because of the nature of our work, we often spent a tremendous amount of time behind a computer screen, thus makes it hard to go to the gym everyday. That's why we built Go Fit to help others who are busy can still work out at home, at the park or even during their lunch breaks. We want to help to promote healthy lifestyle across the globe. So no matter who you are or what you do, as long as you have 30 minutes and a computer, Go Fit will helps you achieve your Fitness goals.
